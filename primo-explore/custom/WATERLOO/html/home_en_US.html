Welcome to Primo with Primo Central!

What is Primo Central?

Primo Central is an index that provides credible, relevance ranked results from many of the Library's online and print collections in a single search.

Primo now includes everything found in the Library's catalogue, plus hundreds of millions of online full-text journal articles available through the Library.

Use Primo to find resources, including:

  • Books
  • Journal articles
  • News articles
  • Items in the Library's catalogue and more

Primo now gives you the option of using 3 different search tabs:

Search

This tab searches books, journal articles and all other resources available through this search tool, including: e-books, journal titles, maps, videos, audio recordings, government documents, music scores, newspaper articles, reviews, and conference proceedings.

Books +

This tab searches books, e-books, journal titles, maps, videos, audio recordings, government documents, and music scores available to University of Guelph, University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University libraries.

Articles +

This tab searches journal articles, newspaper articles, reviews, and conference proceedings available through many of the library's electronic subscriptions.
